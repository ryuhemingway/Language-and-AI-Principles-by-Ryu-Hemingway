# Harnesses and Evaluation

Harnesses make AI systems testable and repeatable.

## Principles Included

- Test cases
- Golden sets
- Rubrics
- Exact checks
- Heuristic checks
- LLM-as-judge
- Regression testing
- Reports and failure analysis

## Why It Matters

AI demos are easy. Reliable AI products require repeatable evaluation.
Harnesses help teams compare prompts, models, tools, and workflows.

## Build Practice

Create `eval.py` that loads test cases, runs your AI workflow, scores outputs,
and writes `results.json`.
